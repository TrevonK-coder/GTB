🛡️ Financial Rules & Agreements

01
Monthly Contribution

Every member contributes KES 3,000 by the 5th of each month. Late payments incur a KES 300 penalty.

02
Investment Lock-In

All collective investments require a minimum 60-day lock-in period before withdrawal discussions.

03
Event Reserve

15% of the treasury balance is reserved for events and emergencies. Not accessible for investments.

04
Profit Sharing

Investment returns are distributed equally (12.5% per member) quarterly, after reinvesting 20% back into the pool.

05
Exit Clause

A departing member forfeits their investment share for the current quarter. Past returns are retained.
