// Step 01

What We Need From You

Send your handles to The Architect so your cards go live. Each member needs up to 3 links.

📸 INSTAGRAM

Your Instagram username — e.g. @chef_kenar. We'll link your member card directly to your profile.

🎵 TIKTOK

Your TikTok handle — e.g. @gtbemzee. Short-form content from your account will appear in the TikTok feed.

▶ YOUTUBE

Your YouTube channel handle or URL — long-form content, vlogs, music drops. Even a playlist link works.
